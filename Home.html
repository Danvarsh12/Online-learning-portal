ABOUT US


Intellipaat is a global online professional training provider that offers certification training programs in a variety of technologies. Their courses include topics such as Big Data, Data Science, and Artificial Intelligence. Intellipaat offers a unique approach to creating course content, and they collaborate with SMEs for training

Here are some of the features of Intellipaat:

  • Classes

Intellipaat offers interactive online training classes that can be attended from anywhere in the world


  • Support
Intellipaat offers learners lifelong support and access to course materials


  • Professional Help
Intellipaat offers professional help to learners, including mock interview sessions and resume assistance.
